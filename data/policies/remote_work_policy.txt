Remote Work Policy

1. Eligibility
- Employees with at least 3 months of service are eligible for remote work.
- The role must be suitable for remote operations.

2. Remote Work Days
- Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week.
- Remote work days must be pre-approved by the manager.

3. Working Hours
- Employees must stay available during core hours (10:00 AMâ€“6:00 PM).
- All meetings must be attended online and on time.

4. Communication
- Employees must remain reachable via email, chat tools, and phone.
- Daily status updates should be shared with the manager.

5. Data Security
- Employees must use company-approved devices and VPN.
- Company data must not be stored or shared through personal accounts.

6. Revocation
- Remote work privileges may be revoked due to misconduct or policy violations.
