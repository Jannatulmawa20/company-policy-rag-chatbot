Conduct & Ethics Policy

1. Integrity
- Employees must act honestly and ethically.
- Fraud, theft, and misrepresentation are strictly prohibited.

2. Respectful Workplace
- Everyone must be treated with respect.
- Harassment, bullying, and discrimination are not tolerated.

3. Conflict of Interest
- Employees must avoid personal interests affecting company decisions.
- Any conflict must be reported to management or HR.

4. Anti-Bribery
- Employees cannot accept or offer bribes or inappropriate gifts.
- Gifts that influence business decisions must be declined.

5. Confidential Information
- Company and client data must remain confidential.
- Unauthorized disclosure is not permitted.

6. Reporting
- Employees are encouraged to report unethical behavior.
- Whistleblowers will be protected.
