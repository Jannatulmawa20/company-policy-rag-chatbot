Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 20 working days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be requested at least 3 days in advance.
- Annual leave requires approval from the reporting manager or HR.

2. Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- Sick leave longer than 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
- Sick leave should be informed before working hours whenever possible.

3. Casual Leave
- Employees may take up to 5 days of casual leave per year for personal or urgent matters.
- Casual leave must be informed at least 1 day ahead, except emergencies.

4. Unpaid Leave
- Employees who need more leave than allowed may request unpaid leave.
- Approval is subject to manager and HR review.

5. Leave Request Process
- All leave requests must be submitted through the HR system or via email.
- HR will maintain the leave records for all employees.
